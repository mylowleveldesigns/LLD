Factory Pattern -> used for object creation based on conditions
Conditional basis pe objects creation needed.

Abstract Factory Pattern ->
It's a factory of factory
Eg We need vehicle.
There are 2 types of vehicles -> Ordinary Vehicle, Luxury Vehicle.

There can be two factories for getting vehicles -> OrdinaryVehicleFactory and LuxuryVehicleFactory.

Now we will again have a factory which will provide either of the above two.
